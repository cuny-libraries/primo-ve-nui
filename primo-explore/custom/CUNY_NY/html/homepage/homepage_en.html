What am I searching?

This discovery tool searches in one place for books, articles, DVDs, CDs, and more.

It allows you to search the CUNY catalog, and many of the library's databases with one interface.

Start out with OneSearch if you:

  • are looking for a specific title, whether an article or a book,
  • want a combination of books, articles, ebooks, etc. on a topic, or
  • are just starting your research.

If you have a specific topic:

  • get more specific sources by searching one of our specialized databases, your subject area's research guides, or browse through our online journals.
