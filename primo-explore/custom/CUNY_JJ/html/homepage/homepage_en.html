Welcome to OneSearch

This discovery tool lets you search in one place for books, articles, videos, and more.

How do I...

  • Find books?
  • Find articles?
  • Choose search terms?
  • Request books from other CUNY libraries?

Use OneSearch if you:

  • are looking for a specific article or a book
  • want to find many books, articles, videos, and more on a topic

If you are just starting your research:

  • try our First-Year Friendly Sources
  • explore our Library Basics guide

For comprehensive research on a specific topic:

  • get more specific sources by searching in specialized databases
  • consult your subject area's research guides
  • search for journal titles
Ask a Librarian

Lloyd Sealy Librarians are happy to assist students and faculty with their research needs. John Jay College community members may communicate with us by chat, email, phone, or in person.
