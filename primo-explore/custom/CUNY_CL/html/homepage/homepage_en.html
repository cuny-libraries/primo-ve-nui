What am I searching?

CUNY Law OneSearch helps you find print books, e-books, articles, DVDs, and more, all in one search result.

CUNY Law OneSearch locates these materials by simultaneously searching: 1) the CUNY library catalog; 2) a massive index of articles from journals, magazines, and newspapers; 3) unique digital content from the library.

 Is OneSearch right for me?

Use OneSearch if you:

  • are looking for a specific title, whether an article or a book,
  • want a combination of books, articles, ebooks, etc. on a topic, or
  • are just starting your research.

However, if you:

  • want specialized legal sources, search one of our legal databases or other legal research sources.
  • want general, non-legal sources, search one of our non-legal databases or other non-legal research sources.

How do I use OneSearch?

After you've entered a basic or advanced search, OneSearch provides numerous options to refine your results, including by type of resource, subject, date, and more. You also have the option to sign in using in your library barcode number so you can save searches and items you've found.
