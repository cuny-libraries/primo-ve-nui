How does this work?

OneSearch provides simple, one-stop searching for books and ebooks, videos, articles, digital media, and more.

How to get started:
  • Type in the title, author or keywords you are looking for in the search box above.
  • Sign in using your CUNYFirst login for full results from our databases or to make requests for books or articles.
  • By default, you are searching for Kingsborough resources, but you can also expand to other CUNY and SUNY libraries.

Not finding what you need? We have subject databases that might help you find articles in a specific area of study. Find the list here.
