What you can and can't find in OneSearch

Books, ebooks, and DVDs at Hostos

Books, ebooks, and DVDs at other CUNY colleges

The default for OneSearch is to look at Hostos first. If you want to search all CUNY libraries, just click on the tab above that says "All CUNY."

Articles from journals, newspapers, and magazines

Many—but not all—journal, magazine and newspaper articles are available through OneSearch. You must sign in with your library barcode (the long number on the back of your Hostos ID) to see the most results.

Books at non-CUNY libraries

For books outside of CUNY, you can search thousands of libraries through Worldcat, which will show you the nearest libraries (both college and public libraries) that have the book you want.

Articles from reference books

Reference articles are a great place to start your research, but they will be easier to find in our Gale eBooks.
