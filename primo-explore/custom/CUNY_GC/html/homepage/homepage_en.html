OneSearch is a great place to start your research. Here's what you will and won't find:

Books @ CUNY

OneSearch includes print books from all CUNY libraries.

Ebooks @ Graduate Center

OneSearch includes ebooks from over 30 ebook collections.

Reserves @ Graduate Center

To find items kept on reserve at the library, see Course Reserves.

Articles @ Graduate Center

Many (but not all) journal, magazine and newspaper articles are available through OneSearch. If you are off campus, sign in to see additional results. For individual databases, which are often better suited to advanced research, consult the library's A-Z list of databases or research guides.

Dissertations @ CUNY and Beyond

OneSearch includes dissertations from CUNY and many other universities. You may also wish to search CUNY Academic Works, Dissertations & Theses Global, and other dissertation databases.

Books @ Other Libraries

Use WorldCat to search for books at thousands of libraries worldwide. Request items using the "Request through Interlibrary Loan" feature.
