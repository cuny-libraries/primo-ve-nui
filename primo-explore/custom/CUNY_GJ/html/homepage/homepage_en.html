What am I searching?

OneSearch at Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism at CUNY lets you search in one place for books, articles, DVDs, CDs, and more.

OneSearch combines in one searchable place these three things: the CUNY library catalog; a massive index of articles from journals, magazines, and newspapers; and unique digital content from the library.

 What can/can't I find in OneSearch?

Books

Print books & e-books at the J-School and other CUNY colleges are discoverable via OneSearch. (E-books at other CUNY colleges can be found, too, but access will be restricted to local users.)

Articles

You can use OneSearch to find scholarly journal articles, magazine articles, and newspaper articles.

Media

Streaming media and DVDs at the J-School is available via OneSearch. (You may be able to find media from other CUNY colleges but access is restricted to local users.)

E-Reserves

Material on electronic reserve is only available on our E-Reserves page.

Data, Statistics, Reports

You'll have better luck using one of our specialized resources on our Databases A-Z page.
