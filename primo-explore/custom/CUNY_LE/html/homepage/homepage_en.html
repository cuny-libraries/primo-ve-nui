What am I searching?

OneSearch lets you search in one place for books, textbooks, ebooks, articles, newspapers, media, and more.

Most (but not all) of the Library's databases are in OneSearch.

You can search Lehman or all CUNY Libraries or just Reserve items.

 Is OneSearch right for me?

OneSearch is a great tool for searching multiple library resources from one interface.

 To go deeper into subject research:
  • Consult the Research Guides in your major or subject area. From there you will find databases that are specific to your research needs.
  • Consult the Library's Databases A-Z and By Subject.
OneSearch is a great starting point:
  • To search for specific books or articles
  • To search on a broad topic looking for ideas or general background
  • To get a view of many types of resources available on a topic
